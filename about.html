Why We Built Nave

Catholic communities are everywhere — parishes, schools, missionaries, retreats, businesses — but they're rarely visible. Finding your Catholic neighborhood shouldn't require word of mouth, Google searches, and guesswork. Nave is the key to connecting Catholic community.

About the Founders

Head of Product

When deciding where to raise a family, one thing became clear: proximity to strong Catholic community matters. But finding that community — the right parish, school, and network of support — proved surprisingly difficult. From young adults leaving campus ministry to families putting down roots, Nave helps Catholics find where they belong.

Head of Go-to-Market

Missionary life with FOCUS meant journeying with students and families through the highs and lows of faith. But one challenge kept surfacing: helping people find where they belong in the Church. Whether in a foreign country or a new neighborhood, the search for Catholic community shouldn't be a barrier to encountering Christ. Nave removes that barrier.
