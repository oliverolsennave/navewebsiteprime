Why We Built This

Catholic communities are everywhere — parishes, schools, missionaries, retreats, businesses — but they're rarely visible. Finding your Catholic neighborhood shouldn't require word of mouth, Google searches, and guesswork. Nave maps the dots so you can connect them.

We're not the Benedict Option — we're coming for Philadelphia and San Francisco.

About the Founders

Head of Product

When deciding where to raise a family, one thing became clear: proximity to strong Catholic community matters. But finding that community — the right parish, school, and network of support — proved surprisingly difficult. Nave was built to help families make informed decisions about where to put down roots and grow together in faith.

Head of Go-to-Market

Seven years as an international missionary with the Fellowship of Catholic University Students (FOCUS) revealed a consistent challenge: helping people find their place in the Church. From language barriers to unfamiliar parish structures, the difficulty of connecting newcomers to Catholic community became the driving force behind Nave.
