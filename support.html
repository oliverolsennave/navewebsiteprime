Frequently Asked Questions

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Open your iPhone Settings, tap your name at the top, then tap Subscriptions. Find Nave and manage or cancel from there. Subscriptions must be cancelled at least 24 hours before the renewal date.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Home tab in the app, tap Settings, and select "Delete Account." Your data will be removed within 30 days. You can also email privacy@catholicnave.com to request deletion.

How do I update my parish or business listing?

Navigate to your listing on the map, tap the pencil icon to enter edit mode, and use the + button to add photos, events, staff, and more.

Is Gabriel AI official Catholic teaching?

No. Gabriel is an AI assistant that helps you discover Catholic resources. Its responses are for informational purposes only and should not be considered official Church teaching or a substitute for professional advice.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds for in-app purchases are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund through Apple.
