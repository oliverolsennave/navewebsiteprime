Welcome to Nave ("we," "our," or "us"). Nave is a mobile application designed to help Catholics discover parishes, Catholic businesses, schools, pilgrimages, retreats, and other faith-based resources.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Services: Operate the app, display nearby Catholic resources, and process your requests
  • Personalize Experience: Remember your preferences and customize content
  • Process Subscriptions: Manage Nave Pro subscriptions and provide premium features
  • Improve Our Services: Analyze usage patterns and fix bugs
  • Communicate: Send service-related announcements, respond to inquiries, and provide customer support
  • Ensure Safety: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our terms
  • AI Features: Process queries through our Gabriel AI assistant to provide relevant Catholic resource suggestions

4. Gabriel AI Assistant

How Gabriel Works

Gabriel is an AI-powered assistant that helps you discover Catholic resources. When you submit a question to Gabriel, your query is sent to our servers and processed using third-party AI services. We use information from our database of Catholic resources to generate contextual responses. Your location may be used to provide geographically relevant suggestions.

AI Data Practices

  • No Training: Your individual queries are not used to train AI models
  • Temporary Processing: Queries are processed in real-time and not permanently stored with personally identifiable information
  • Query Limits: We track the number of queries for rate limiting and subscription features, but not the content of queries
  • Accuracy Disclaimer: AI responses are for informational purposes only. Gabriel may make mistakes and responses should not be considered official Catholic Church teaching or professional advice

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for up to 30 days after deletion

Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through the app's Settings. Upon deletion, your profile information will be removed within 30 days. Content you submitted may be anonymized or removed. Some information may be retained as required by law.
